About Palmdé

At Palmdé, we envision a time when every company choice is based on sustainability. Our goal is to enable both individuals and businesses to make environmentally responsible decisions. We are dedicated to providing cutting-edge solutions that support sustainable living, minimise energy use, and reduce waste.

Founded in 2024, Palmdé was born out of a passion for nature and a desire to combat climate change through innovation. Our team of experts is dedicated to creating lasting change, one sustainable solution at a time.
